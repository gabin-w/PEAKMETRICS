01

Market Context

PeakMetrics operates at the intersection of the sports nutrition ($61 billion) and HealthTech markets. In 2026, the dominant trend is "Data-Driven Wellness" — consumers demand solutions grounded in biological evidence, not generic formulas. The proliferation of connected wearables (watches, rings) creates a unique window for real-time adaptive nutrition.

02

The Unique Opportunity

The problem is the "blind supplement" — 90% of amateur athletes consume supplements that are mismatched to their biology, leading to ineffectiveness or dangerous overdose. Fear of contamination with banned substances further holds back serious athletes. PeakMetrics eliminates guesswork with biologically validated, anti-doping certified formulas.

Enabling every athlete to reach their full potential through precision, biologically personalized and scalable nutrition, rooted in scientific rigor and full transparency.
